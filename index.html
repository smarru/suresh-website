xsede.org

The Extreme Science and Engineering Discovery Environment (XSEDE) is a single virtual system that scientists can use to interactively share computing resources, data and expertise. People around the world use these resources and services — things like supercomputers, collections of data and new tools — to improve our planet.

sciencegateways.org

SGCI provide NSF-funded, online and in-person resources and services. their goal is to facilitate—at little or no cost—the sharing of experiences, technologies, and practices of those working with science gateways. They've supported gateway development and community of gateway creators, users, and students.

Apache Software Foundation
and Airavata

Suresh Marru is a Nominated Member of the Apache Software Foundation and co-founder and the Vice-President of Apache Airavata.
Suresh is an avid advocate of applying meritocratic governance principles to open source communities and focuses on research topics at the intersection of application domain science; computational and distributed systems and has authored or co-authored over 75 peer-reviewed conference papers and journal articles in these areas.

Suresh is co-teaching a class on building science gateway architectures and has extensive speaking experience at various conferences including ApacheCons.
